Explore Safety Map here!

Walking alone in the dark? Browse the UCSC safety map to locate popular walking paths and well-light areas to guide you along a safe journey home. Check out the key below for reference.

1. Highlighted RED numerical regions indicate crucial well-lit landmarks and higher frequency of lighted paths overall.
2. Highlighted BLUE numerical regions indicate possible well-light regions and lower frequency of lighted paths overall.
3. Zoom into the map to take note of key well-light and popular walking areas.
4. Click on a pinpoint to see indication of lampost at selected point. 5. Select bus stop pin points to see which buses travel to selected bus stop (including the Loop Bus).
